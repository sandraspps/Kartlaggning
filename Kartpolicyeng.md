# Privacy Policy

## About the App


NeedsMapping is an app for anonymous assessment and follow-up of individual abilities, strengths, difficulties and support needs.


The app is designed for use in support services and other organizations where systematic documentation and follow-up is important.

## Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the app handles information and protects user and individual privacy. The app is designed for anonymous assessment and follow-up of individual abilities, strengths, difficulties and support needs, with privacy and data minimization as fundamental principles.

2. Fundamental Principles

The app is built to:

Not collect personal data

Not identify individuals

Not share information automatically

Give the user full control over all stored information

All use of the app is at the user's own responsibility and in accordance with applicable legislation.

3. Anonymized Information
All information handled in the app should be anonymized.

Individuals are identified only by optional ID, such as a number or code
The app should not be used to register names, personal ID numbers, addresses or other directly or indirectly identifying information

The app does not require or request personal data

The purpose is to enable assessment without any individual being identifiable.

4. What Information is Handled
The app only handles information that the user chooses to enter, such as:

Assessments and follow-ups across different life and function areas

Ratings via scales and selectable options

Free-text professional comments and observations

Dates for assessments and follow-ups

No information is collected automatically.

5. Information Storage
All information is stored in the app's database

No information is shared with third parties

No information is transferred externally unless the user actively chooses to export or share it

The user is responsible for how exported information is stored and handled after sharing.

6. Sharing and Export

The user can choose to:

Export summaries as PDF

Send summaries via email

When sharing, the user is responsible for ensuring the information is handled securely and legally by the recipient.

7. No Tracking or Analytics

The app:

Does not use cookies

Does not use third-party analytics tools

Does not track user behavior

Does not collect technical identifying information

8. User Responsibility

   
### Important About Anonymity and Security

It is the user's full responsibility to ensure that no information that can be traced to specific individuals is entered in the app. This includes, but is not limited to: names, personal ID numbers, addresses, specific workplaces, names of healthcare providers or facilities, and other direct or indirect identifying information.

The user is responsible for:

Only using anonymized codes and descriptions

Not entering names of people, healthcare providers, workplaces or specific places

For legal guardians, only entering type (guardian/trustee/personal representative), not names

For healthcare contacts, only entering type of care, not specific facilities or staff

Using the app on a secure and protected device

Storing the device securely and using appropriate password protection

Not sharing access to the app with unauthorized persons

Securely deleting data if the device is to be decommissioned or change owner

9. Legal Assessment

The app is designed for use without processing personal data under data protection legislation. Provided the user only uses anonymized data, the use is not covered by personal data processing rules.

If the app is used in a way that involves entering personal data, it is the user or organization that is responsible for ensuring applicable legislation is followed.

10. Security
The app is designed with focus on:

Data minimization

Limited information access

Clear structure and overview

The user is responsible for protecting their device and ensuring unauthorized persons do not gain access to the app.

11. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated with changes to the app's functionality or changed legal requirements. The latest version is always available in the app.

12. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the app's information handling, please contact the app's responsible developer.
