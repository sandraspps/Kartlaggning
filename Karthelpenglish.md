# Help & Guide


Learn how to use NeedsMapping


### Get started quickly

1

Create a person
Tap “New person” on the home screen. Use an anonymous ID (e.g. A1234).

2

Create a mapping
Go to the person’s page and tap “New mapping”. Assess the life areas.

3

Follow up over time
Create new mappings to track progress. Compare results and export as PDF.

⸻

### Assessment Scale

The scale shows the level of support needed (0–4)

0 Manages independently – No support needed

1 Minor support – Needs some help or reminders

2 Moderate support – Needs regular assistance

3 Extensive support – Needs a high level of assistance

4 Cannot manage without support – Fully dependent on assistance

⸻

### App Features

Person Management

Create, edit, and delete people using anonymous IDs

Needs Mapping

Document all 13 life areas with detailed assessments

Progress Charts

Visualize changes over time with interactive charts

Export & Reports

Export as PDF, print, or save backups

⸻

### Privacy

All data is stored locally on the device. No data is shared with third parties.

⸻
